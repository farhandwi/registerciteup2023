Science of Logical Computation

The Logic Competition is held in a team consisting of a maximum of 2 people or individually. Each participant will receive a race manual as well as a group of participants along with the race committee. The competition will be held in two stages, namely the preliminary round and the final round. The preliminary round will be the elimination stage for the participants leaving as many as 20 of the participants who took part in the competition to go to the final round. The final round will determine which participants win 1st, 2nd and 3rd place. The preliminary round will be carried out online using a zoom meeting and the final round will be held offline at Pertamina University. Competition registration will be carried out in three phases in stages.

Poster Design

The Poster Competition is a national-level competition that all high school/vocational school level students can participate in as part of a series of events at CITE UP 6 2023. This competition is a competition to make creative works in the form of posters that are made as attractive as possible and easy to understand with visualization media. In this competition, participants will make a design in the form of a poster with a theme determined by the committee. The poster design is creative and innovative. The Poster design competition field will provide an opportunity for participants to express their creativity in conveying information, according to supporting data in a visual form. Each participant will receive a race manual as well as a group of participants along with the race committee. The competition will be held in two stages, namely preliminary and final. The preliminary stage is the selection of works after the works have been collected from the participants and then the best work will be assessed to enter the final round. Participants who enter the final round will carry out finalist presentations to find 1st, 2nd and 3rd place winners. All works of finalists will be displayed and voted on by seminar participants to find their favorite winner. Competition registration will be carried out in three batches according to the specified schedule.
